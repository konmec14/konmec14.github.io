Dietersdorf

The lovely village called Dietersdorf is part of the lovely country Austria. And there is indeed some interesting facts about this tiny small village. Not only are there not even 500 inhabitants, the size only adds up to 6,56 square kilometers!

Music

In this village there is also a Marching Band with just over 30 participants. If you are interested in that kind of music you might like to visit the home page of this Marching band, therefor just click on the town coat of arms.
If you don't like their homepage you may like their Facebook-Page.

Pizza

You are in Dietersdorf and want some pizza? No problem! Heuboden will make the most delicious pizzas you can find near here. But not only pizza can be found there. There is also other italian food. Pasta, Shrimps, tagliatelle, you name it!

Sports

And if you are bored, you can just go to the soccer field and play soccer. There is even a possibility to play some volleyball. And the best thing is, that just 5 minutes into the woods there is a model airfield!
