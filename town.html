Dietersdorf

The lovely village called Dietersdorf is part of the lovely country Austria. And there is indeed some interestingt data about this tiny small village. Not only are there not even 500 inhabitants, the size only adds up to 6,56 square kilometers!

Music

In this village there is also a Marching Band with just over 30 participants. If you are interested in that kind of music you might like to visit the HomePage of this Marching band or even their Facebook-Page.
